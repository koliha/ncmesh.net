    • NC Mesh

    NC Mesh is a community group working to build a mesh network of grid and solar powered meshtastic radios across North Carolina. This network acts like a regional text messaging system, allowing people to communicate publicly or privately with anyone else on the network.

    Join the mesh

    More information

    Please see our Meshtastic ELI5 and a great list of tips for starting your own mesh network. Take a look through the navigation at the top of our site and feel free to join us on discord. We'd love to hear from you!
